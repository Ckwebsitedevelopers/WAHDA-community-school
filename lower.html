Our Grades 1-4 program builds strong literacy and numeracy skills while incorporating creative arts, physical education, and social development activities.

Drawing, painting, music, dance, and drama to foster self-expression.

Physical Education

Developing motor skills, teamwork, and sportsmanship through games and exercises.
