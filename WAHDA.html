About Our School

Discover what makes Wahda Community School a special place for your child's early education journey in Ng'ombe Compound.

Welcome to Wahda Community School

Established to serve the Ng'ombe community, Wahda Community School has been providing quality foundational education since our founding. Our mission is to create a nurturing environment where young learners develop a love for learning, build strong character, and acquire essential skills for their educational journey.

With dedicated teachers who understand early childhood development, we create an environment where every child can thrive and discover their potential.

Child-Centered Learning

Our approach focuses on each child's individual needs and learning style.

Foundational Curriculum

We build strong literacy and numeracy skills through engaging activities.

Community Focus

Deeply rooted in Ng'ombe Compound, serving local families.

Happy Environment

We create a joyful space where children love to learn.

School Uniform

Our distinctive uniform creates a sense of belonging and pride in our school community.

Our Uniform Policy

All students at Wahda Community School are required to wear our official uniform, which consists of a light blue shirt and navy blue pants/skirt. The uniform must be worn clean and neat at all times.

Wearing the uniform helps students develop pride in their school and creates an atmosphere of equality and focus on learning.

Shirt

Light blue short/long sleeve shirt with school logo

Bottoms

Navy blue trousers for boys, navy blue skirts/pinafores for girls

Shoes

Black closed shoes with navy blue socks

Sweater

Navy blue sweater with school logo (optional in cold weather)

Our Facilities

We provide child-friendly facilities designed to support early learning and development.

Reading Corner

Our colorful reading area is filled with age-appropriate books to foster early literacy and a love for reading.

Creative Arts

Dedicated spaces for art, music, and drama allow children to express themselves creatively.

Play Area

Safe outdoor and indoor play spaces with educational toys and equipment for physical development.

Dining Area

Clean, supervised spaces for children to enjoy their meals and learn healthy eating habits.
