Welcome to Wahda Community School

Founded in 2005, Wahda Community School has been at the forefront of providing quality education that combines academic rigor with character development. Our mission is to empower students to become critical thinkers, lifelong learners, and responsible global citizens.

With a team of dedicated educators and state-of-the-art facilities, we create an environment where every child can thrive and reach their full potential.

Our Facilities

We provide modern, well-equipped facilities to support effective teaching and learning across all areas of the curriculum.

Library

Our extensive library features thousands of books, digital resources, and quiet study spaces to foster a love of reading and research.

Science Labs

Modern, well-equipped laboratories for physics, chemistry, and biology allow students to conduct experiments and explore scientific concepts.

Computer Lab

Our technology center provides students with access to computers and software to develop digital literacy and coding skills.

Sports Complex

The sports facilities include a football field, basketball court, swimming pool, and indoor gym to promote physical fitness and teamwork.
